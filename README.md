# ⏱️ Average Handling Time (AHT) Improvement Using Six Sigma DMAIC

A Six Sigma Green Belt project focused on reducing Average Handling Time (AHT) by eliminating process inefficiencies, streamlining troubleshooting workflows and improving customer support performance through the DMAIC methodology.

---

## Project Overview

This project aimed to reduce customer interaction handling time while maintaining service quality and improving the overall support experience. By analyzing operational workflows, identifying non-value-adding activities and implementing standardized support practices, the project improved operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.

---

## Business Problem

High Average Handling Time (AHT) resulted in reduced operational efficiency, increased agent workload and inconsistent customer experiences. The objective was to eliminate unnecessary process steps and improve support effectiveness without compromising service quality.

---

## Objectives

- Reduce Average Handling Time (AHT).
- Improve customer satisfaction.
- Eliminate non-value-adding activities.
- Standardize troubleshooting workflows.
- Establish sustainable process controls.

---

## DMAIC Methodology

### Define

- Defined project scope, objectives and key performance indicators (KPIs).
- Identified Average Handling Time (AHT) as the primary improvement metric.

### Measure

- Collected baseline operational data.
- Audited over **200 customer calls and chat interactions**.
- Mapped end-to-end support processes using SIPOC diagrams and Time Motion Studies.

### Analyze

- Applied Pareto Analysis to identify the largest contributors to high AHT.
- Used Fishbone Diagram and 5 Whys to determine root causes.
- Identified redundant validation steps, inefficient troubleshooting paths and inconsistent support practices.

### Improve

- Streamlined troubleshooting workflows.
- Standardized Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs).
- Conducted targeted coaching and training for support agents.

### Control

- Implemented dashboards and Control Charts.
- Continuously monitored AHT and customer satisfaction KPIs.
- Established process controls to sustain long-term improvements.

---

## Key Business Outcomes

- Reduced Average Handling Time (AHT) by **18%**.
- Improved Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) by **15%**.
- Reduced agent workload through more efficient support processes.
- Improved consistency across customer interactions.
- Increased operational efficiency through standardized workflows.
